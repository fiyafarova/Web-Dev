Chocolate Cake

  • Time: 1 hour 30 minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Flour 2 cups
Sugar 1.5 cups
Cocoa powder 0.5 cup
Eggs 3
Butter 1 cup

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease the cake pan.

Step 2: Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.

Step 3: Beat in eggs and melted butter until smooth.

Step 4: Pour the batter into the pan and bake for 30–35 minutes.

Pasta Salad

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Pasta 2 cups
Cherry tomatoes 1 cup
Cucumber 1, chopped
Feta cheese 0.5 cup
Olive oil 2 tbsp

Step 1: Cook pasta according to package instructions and let cool.

Step 2: Chop vegetables and mix with pasta.

Step 3: Add feta cheese and drizzle with olive oil.

Step 4: Toss everything together and serve chilled.
